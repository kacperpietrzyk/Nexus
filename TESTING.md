# Testing Nexus

## Join the TestFlight beta
You need an Apple ID; minimum iOS 26 / macOS 26.
- **macOS:** https://testflight.apple.com/join/YBxhZ7SQ
- **iOS / iPadOS:** https://testflight.apple.com/join/cQ97NSzW

(External testing is reviewed by Apple per build, so a new build may take a short
while to become installable.)

## Giving feedback
- In TestFlight: take a screenshot → "Share Beta Feedback", or report a crash (auto-sent).
- Bugs / ideas: open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/kacperpietrzyk/Nexus/issues/new/choose).
- Questions: [Discussions](https://github.com/kacperpietrzyk/Nexus/discussions).

## What to try first
Quick capture (⌘N on Mac), the Today view, Inbox, marking tasks done, editing a task,
natural-language dates ("tomorrow 5pm", "jutro 17:00"). Tell us what's confusing or missing.
