# Governance

Nexus is maintainer-led.

## Roles
- **Maintainer** (@kacperpietrzyk): final say on scope, merges, releases.
- **Contributors**: anyone with a merged PR. Trusted contributors may be granted triage.

## How decisions are made
- Scope must fit the single-user product (see `docs/architecture.md`). Out-of-scope ideas are
  declined with a pointer to that doc.
- Changes land via PR with at least one approving review and green CI. The maintainer may
  request changes or decline.
- Larger features: open a Discussion or issue first to agree on the approach before coding.

## Becoming a co-maintainer
Sustained, high-quality contributions + good review judgment → the maintainer may invite you.
